\begin{exercisebox}{Exercise 4}
Let $E$ be a nonempty subset of an ordered set; suppose $\alpha$ is a lower bound of E and $\beta$ is an upper bound of $E$. Prove that $\alpha \leq \beta$
\end{exercisebox}

\textbf{Solution}

Let $ x \in E$. From the statement, we have $x\leq \beta$ and $\alpha \leq x$, this implies $\alpha \leq \beta$. $\blacksquare$
